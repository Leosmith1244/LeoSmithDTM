About Leo Smith, DTM

Distinguished Toastmaster • Leadership Mentor • Inspirational Speaker

My Leadership Journey

Leo Smith, DTM, is a distinguished leader, communicator, and mentor with a passion for empowering individuals and organizations through effective communication and leadership. As a Distinguished Toastmaster (DTM), the highest educational achievement in Toastmasters International, Leo has demonstrated exceptional dedication to personal growth and the advancement of others.

With extensive experience in various leadership roles within Toastmasters District 106, including past positions as a club officer and district leader, Leo has guided numerous clubs to achieve excellence through the Distinguished Club Program (DCP).

A dynamic public speaker, Leo captivates audiences with engaging, purpose-driven speeches that inspire action and foster collaboration. His expertise spans crafting compelling narratives, delivering impactful presentations, and mentoring emerging leaders to unlock their potential.

Professional Impact

As the Public Relations Manager for District 106, alongside District Director Matthew Villella, Leo has spearheaded innovative initiatives, such as workshops tailored for workforce skill development, blending Toastmasters' proven methodologies with modern leadership frameworks like the 3C Leadership model: Communicate, Collaborate, Catalyze.

Beyond Toastmasters, Leo is a seasoned professional whose career is marked by a commitment to community engagement and professional excellence. His leadership extends to designing high-impact seminars, including a $4,500 leadership workshop that equips businesses with actionable strategies for today's dynamic workplace.

Known for his approachable yet authoritative style, Leo combines strategic insight with a genuine passion for helping others succeed. Whether leading a club to new heights, mentoring a first-time speaker, or driving district-wide growth, Leo Smith embodies the heart of a thriving Toastmasters community, leaving a lasting impact on all who have the privilege of working with him.
