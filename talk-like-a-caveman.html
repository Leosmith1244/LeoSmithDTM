Leo Program

Best audience

Toastmasters clubs, speaker-development groups, trainers, leaders, and anyone who overcomplicates a message.

Audience takeaways

  • 1. Use simpler language without sounding simplistic.
  • 2. Find the one thing the audience must remember.
  • 3. Trade foggy polish for concrete images.
  • 4. Build a message people can repeat later.

Why this works

A good speech needs more than a topic. It needs a hook, a promise, a path, and a useful landing.

Hook A title and metaphor people remember.
Story Human examples instead of corporate fog.
Tool A practical idea the audience can use.
Action A clear next step before they leave.
