Amplifying Your Impact: Digital PR for Toastmasters

Room 3
July 26, 2024 • 30 minutes
Facilitator: Leo Smith, DTM

Empower VPPRs to leverage digital tools for club promotion, focusing on social media and measurable outcomes.

Session Agenda

0:00–3:00

Welcome and Icebreaker: "Meme Your Club"

Activity: Participants pair up and describe a funny or inspiring club moment that could be a meme (e.g., "When you nail your first speech!"). Facilitator shares a sample Toastmasters meme.

Purpose: Break the ice, introduce digital PR in a fun way, and highlight shareable content.

Materials: Optional phone/projector to show a meme example.
3:00–9:00

Mini-Presentation: Digital PR Essentials

Content:

  • Why digital PR matters: Reaches wider audiences, builds credibility, and drives guest attendance.
  • Key platforms: FreeToastHost, Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram, X.
  • Best practices: Consistent branding, engaging visuals, clear calls to action (e.g., "Join our next meeting!").
  • Example: A sample Instagram post with a member testimonial and meeting details.

Fun Twist: Show a "bad" social media post (e.g., blurry photo, no details) vs. a "great" one to spark laughs and learning.

Materials: Slides or handouts with platform tips and Canva.com link.
9:00–17:00

Interactive Activity: Create a Social Media Post

Activity:

  • In pairs, participants draft a social media post (text + idea for visual) to promote an upcoming club meeting.
  • Use a template: "[Engaging hook]! Join [Club Name] on [date/time] to [benefit]. [Call to action]."
  • Example: "Nervous about public speaking? Join PRPRO Toastmasters on July 30th to gain confidence in a supportive community. RSVP now!"
  • Pairs share posts; group picks top two for creativity via show of hands.

Purpose: Hands-on practice with digital PR, fostering creativity and teamwork.

Materials: Template handouts, pens; optional prize (e.g., sticker) for top posts.
17:00–25:00

Hands-On Practice: Digital PR Action Plan

Activity:

  • Each participant writes a 3-step digital PR plan (e.g., "Post 1x/week on X, create Canva flyer, track guest RSVPs").
  • Volunteers (2–3) share plans at the front; facilitator provides quick feedback.
  • Tip: Highlight free tools like Canva and Google Analytics for tracking engagement.

Purpose: Encourage actionable takeaways, practiced publicly for confidence.

Materials: Action plan template, timer.
25:00–30:00

Wrap-Up and Action Commitment

Content:

  • Recap key takeaway: Digital PR amplifies your club's reach with simple tools.
  • Action step: Create and schedule one social media post within 48 hours.
  • Fun close: Group selfie with a "#ToastmastersPR" sign for participants to share online.
Materials: Handout with digital PR checklist, sign for selfie.
